Visual Hierarchy

Fight the New Drug:

fightthenewdrug.org

This site demonstrates good Visual Hierarchy in that each section has a clear, bold heading, and an obvious flow. It's easy to follow and there's no question where the eye should head next.

Repetition

Hands and Voices:

handsandvoices.org

This website shows good Repetition in that each section has a header, then a paragraph, followed by a "Learn More" button, and ends width an image.

White Space and Clean Design

Zillow

zillow.com

This website is a good example of a Clean Design with a good amount of White Space. It is very simple, but still holds the attention. Between the simplistic design, the color palette, and the well-balanced amount of white space, it is a very calming and pleasant page to look at.
